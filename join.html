Graduate Students

PhD Position in Mosquito Population Genomics

We seek a motivated PhD student interested in evolutionary genomics, bioinformatics, and vector biology. Join us to work on cutting-edge research at the intersection of genomics and public health.

Research Focus Areas

Insecticide Resistance

Track resistance evolution using genomic surveillance

Photoperiodic Diapause

Evolution of overwintering strategies

Vector Competence

Genetic basis of pathogen transmission

Population Genomics

GWAS and invasion history reconstruction

How to Apply

Application Deadline: December 1st, 2025 for Fall 2026 admission

Application Process:

  1. Coming Soon: A Google form will be available for candidates interested in the position
  2. Initial Review: After filling out the form, selected students will be contacted for a Zoom interview
  3. Formal Application: Successful candidates must apply through the UCR Entomology Graduate Program and fulfill all departmental requirements
  4. Campus Interview: Selected candidates residing in the USA will be invited for an on-campus interview (typically held in February-March)
